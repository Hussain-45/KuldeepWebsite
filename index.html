Grassroots Foundation (Age 5 - 8)

Fun-first football combined with motor skill training. Focus on spatial awareness, coordination, and building a genuine passion for the game through small-sided games and high touches on the ball.

📅

Weekly Training

3 sessions per week (1 hour each). Emphasizes movement mechanics, fun ball touches, and motor coordination.

🏆

Leagues & Matches

Local baby leagues and recreational weekend football festivals. Stress-free environment focused on participation.

🎯

Technical Focus

1v1 dribbling, basic footwork, running with the ball, and building confidence in close-quarters play.

Youth Development Phase (Age 9 - 13)

Structured, skill-focused development. Introduction to positioning, basic tactical roles, game understanding, and individual technique refinement under pressure from defenders.

📅

Weekly Training

4 sessions per week (1.5 hours each) plus competitive matchday. Refinement of individual technique under pressure.

🏆

Leagues & Matches

Regional youth association leagues, inter-academy matches, and school tournaments.

🎯

Tactical Focus

Team positioning, passing combinations, game understanding, and basic transition play (attack to defense).

Elite Academy Phase (Age 14 - 18)

Professional tactical preparation. High-intensity competitive matches, athletic development, advanced tactical systems, and exposure to ISL scout clinics and national team trials.

📅

Weekly Training

5 sessions per week (2 hours each) plus professional video analysis and strength & conditioning sessions.

🏆

Leagues & Matches

AIFF Youth Leagues (national level), State Super Division, and elite showcase tournaments.

🎯

Professional Path

High-press systems, GPS-tracked physical load management, direct ISL scout exposure, and trial preparation.
