The free trial allows prospective players to participate in a standard age-specific training session. Our coaches will evaluate their skill level, introduce them to the team, and recommend the best development pathway.

All trial players should wear comfortable athletic clothing, shin guards, soccer cleats, and bring a water bottle. We supply training bibs, soccer balls, and professional coaching equipment.
